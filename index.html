How To pLay:

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

  • There are four crystals below and each crystal is assigned a specific amount of points.

    • By clicking on a crystal, you will add its specific amount of points to your total score.

    • The value of each crystal will be hidden from you until you click on it.

  • To win the game:

    • You must match your total score to the random number.

  • You will lose the game if:

    • Your total score goes above the random number.

  • NOTE: Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
