How To pLay:

  • At the start of the game, you will be assigned a randomly generated number (between 19-120).

  • There are four crystals displayed below.

    • Each crystal is randomly assigned a value, or a specific amount of points (between 1-12).

    • The value of each crystal will be hidden from you, until that crystal is clicked.

    • Upon clicking on a crystal, you will add its specific amount of points to your total score.

  • To win the game:

    • You must match your total score to the random number.

  • You will lose the game if:

    • Your total score goes above the random number.


  • NOTE: The game will automatically restart upon winning or losing and each time the game starts, the values of each crystal will also change.
